Financial Statements

Financial Statements are Important Because:

  1. They Show you the Profitability of the Company
  2. They Show the Strengths/Weaknesses of the Company
    • Strengths are Positive
    • Weaknesses are Negative
  3. They allow the Company to Forecast for the Future
